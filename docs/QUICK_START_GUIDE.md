# FeedTan Payment System - Quick Start Guide

Get started with FeedTan in under 10 minutes.

---

## What You'll Need

- Active mobile money account (M-Pesa, Tigo Pesa, or Airtel Money)
- Valid phone number (format: 255XXXXXXXXX)
- Internet connection

---

## Step 1: Access the System

Navigate to: **https://pay.feedtancmg.org**

---

## Step 2: Make Your First Payment

1. **Enter Amount**
   - Type the amount you want to pay in TZS
   - Example: `10000`

2. **Enter Phone Number**
   - Enter your mobile number
   - Format: `255622239304` (no spaces, no + sign)

3. **Enter Your Name**
   - Type your full name
   - Example: `John Doe`

4. **Optional: Add Email**
   - Enter your email to receive payment receipt
   - Example: `john@example.com`

5. **Click "Process Payment"**

---

## Step 3: Confirm Payment on Your Phone

1. **Wait for USSD Prompt**
   - You'll receive a USSD prompt on your phone
   - This usually takes 5-30 seconds

2. **Enter Your PIN**
   - Enter your mobile money PIN
   - This is the same PIN you use for mobile money transactions

3. **Wait for Confirmation**
   - Payment processing takes 30 seconds to 2 minutes
   - You'll see a success message on screen

---

## Step 4: Receive Confirmation

You'll receive:
- **SMS**: Payment confirmation with reference number
- **Email**: Detailed payment receipt (if email was provided)
- **Screen**: Success message with transaction details

---

## Step 5: Check Payment Status (Optional)

1. Go to **https://pay.feedtancmg.org/payment/status**
2. Enter your reference number
3. Click "Check Status"
4. View current payment status

---

## Common Issues

### Payment Not Processing
- **Check**: Phone number format (must be 255XXXXXXXXX)
- **Check**: Sufficient balance in mobile money account
- **Solution**: Retry payment after 2 minutes

### No SMS Received
- **Wait**: SMS may take up to 5 minutes
- **Check**: Phone has network signal
- **Solution**: Check payment status online

### Payment Failed
- **Reason**: Insufficient funds or network timeout
- **Solution**: Top up account and retry payment
